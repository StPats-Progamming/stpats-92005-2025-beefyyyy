Welcome to the official website for the St. Patrick’s College Choir - Conanima

As a passionate and dynamic vocal ensemble, we bring the joy of music to life through harmonies that inspire and uplift. Based in St. Patrick’s College Wellington, our choir is dedicated to musical excellence, community spirit, and the power of song. Whether you’re a lover of music, a potential member for our choir or simply curious about our performances, we invite you to explore our journey as a choir, upcoming events, and rich tradition of choral excellence. Join us as we celebrate the magic of music—one note at a time!
