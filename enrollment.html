Enrollment

Important Information

  1. You must use your School email to register.
  2. Audtions will happen at the start of the year and near the end. Those who are Year 13 can only take auditions at the start of the year.
  3. Registering for the for the choir means your are commiting yourself to the choir to do your part and your best.
  4. Those already in the choir can re-audiotion in the case of changes in your voice throughout the year.
