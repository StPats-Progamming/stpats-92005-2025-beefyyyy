FAQ

Frequently asked questions

  • Q: Who can join the Choir?
    A: Year 10s to Year 13s. For more information check out the Enrollment page.
  • Q: What are the different parts of the choir?
    A: There are 4 parts in our choir in order of lowest to highest. These parts are Bass, Baritone, Tenor 2 and Tenor 1.
  • Q: When will rehersals/practices be?
    A: Every Monday after school from 3:30PM to 5:30PM.
  • Q: Which performances does the choir attend?
    A: The choir attends the anual Big Sing, a nationwide choral festival involving secondary schools across the nation held by the New Zealand Choral Federation. More information at NZCF The Big Sing official webiste.
